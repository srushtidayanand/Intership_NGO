About Us

Janmamithra Trust, established in 2015, inspires youth to share and care for society. With 250+ volunteers in Bengaluru, it has organized 200+ events, including child sponsorships, school adoptions, and monthly meals for the needy. The self-funded Trust now seeks volunteers to help secure organized donations.

Our Vision

To have a world where happiness thrives!

Our Mission

Being constant support to help build a self-reliant community, through selfless contribution and participation by youth, who desire social welfare.
